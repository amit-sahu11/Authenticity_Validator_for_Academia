Demo Helper Credentials for Simulated OCR Verification
======================================================

In Simulated Ledger mode, the OCR engine is pre-programmed to read specific values from the uploaded certificate:

* Student Full Name: Jane Doe
* Enrollment / Roll Number: 1NTU18CS123
* University / Board: Northern Tech University
* Year of Graduation: 2022
* Degree Type: Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

Use the above exact values in the manual entry form to trigger a successful blockchain registration and verification match!
